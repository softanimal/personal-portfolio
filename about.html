MY STORY

I'm a UX/UI designer from Northampton, MA, USA. I have a background in sound design; I created music for film/TV. I started my visual design career designing show posters and album covers for touring bands. After an intensive mentor-guided apprenticeship, I now specialize in User Experience and Visual Design with a focus on universal accessibility. I strive to build effective web and mobile applications people love to use.

I'm looking for new opportunities to design and create, so please feel free to contact me.

I’m a UX/UI designer and digital producer from Northampton, MA, USA. I currently work as a digital coordinator for Smith College. I’m always looking for new opportunities to build web and mobile applications with a heavy focus on universal design and accessibility.

I’ve recently completed an intensive design apprenticeship. Project scopes included user research, user flows and personas, wireframes, branding, high-fidelity prototypes and usability testing. Some of the platforms I regularly work with are Figma/Sketch, Photoshop and Illustrator, Balsamiq, Invision, draw.io, and Marketing Cloud. I have a solid understanding of HTML/CSS, JavaScript syntax, Git/Github and version control.

Some of my early training as a sound designer has enabled me to simultaneously go back and forth from technical to creative, which is precisely the skillset of a digital designer. I graduated from Berklee College of Music with a Bachelor of Arts in sound design. My compositions have been featured on MTV, Hulu, Amazon, Food Network, and HGTV.
